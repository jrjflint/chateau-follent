Suit Selection Guidance

For a Deep Winter wardrobe and maximum formality, prioritise fabrics close to:
• #232328 — Primary recommendation (Executive charcoal)
• #26272C — Everyday cool graphite option
• #1F2024 — Black-adjacent charcoal for eveningwear
Avoid warm anthracites or brown-leaning charcoals that soften the cool, formal effect.

Personal reference for Lord James Follent's suit fabric selection.

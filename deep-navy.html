Suit Selection Guidance

For a Deep Winter wardrobe and maximum versatility, prioritise fabrics close to:
• #1B2452 — Primary recommendation (True Midnight Navy)
• #162447 — Best daily business navy
• #0A1832 — Elevated navy that works for weddings and eveningwear
Avoid royal blues, bright blues, and warm indigo tones.

🍇 The Founding Fellowship of Château Follent

“An exclusive, inaugural circle for those who join the story from the beginning.”

The Founding Fellowship of Château Follent is the first and most honoured tier of the Château Follent membership. Limited to a small company of early believers — a Fellowship in the truest sense — this club represents the original companions who set out on the journey with the House of Follent from its very first vintage.

This isn’t merely a wine club.
It is an order, a circle, a band of founders bound together by craft, story, and the shared belief that great things begin small — with courage, creativity, and good company.

Why the Founding Fellowship Exists

The Founding Fellowship exists to:

  1. Honour those who walk with Château Follent from the very beginning
  2. Offer first and best access to our earliest and most limited releases
  3. Record the names of our companions in the story of the First Vintage

By joining the Fellowship, you are woven into the founding chapter of Château Follent — not as a spectator, but as a recognised companion.

A Limited Company of Twenty-Five

The Fellowship is strictly limited to a small and select company, chosen in the spirit of wisdom, friendship, and shared delight in wine.

  • In this First Age, the company shall number twenty and five (25)
  • These shall be known forever as The Founding Fellowship
  • Once claimed, a place in the Fellowship endures until freely relinquished

Your membership is not just a subscription. It is a seat at the table of the House of Follent in its earliest days.

Membership Benefits

Members of the Founding Fellowship receive a set of privileges designed to reflect their place in the story.

🍷 1. Annual “Chapter Release” Allocation

Each year, members may choose one of two annual allocations:

  • 6 bottles per year – ideal for those who wish to savour the highlights
  • 12 bottles per year – for those who wish to journey deeper into the cellar

Each allocation is drawn from the cellar of Château Follent and is known as your Chapter Release, marking each year of your Fellowship, as vineyards mark the passing of seasons.

💠 2. The Founders’ Advantage (20%)

Founding Fellowship members receive a 20% reduction on the price of all Château Follent wines available to the wider public, for as long as they remain in the Fellowship.

This is our way of acknowledging that you stood with us at the beginning.

🥂 3. Early and Preferred Access

Members of the Founding Fellowship stand first in line for:

  • Limited and single-barrel releases
  • Special cuvées and experimental bottlings
  • Rare or library wines as they are made available

If a wine is scarce, the Fellowship hears of it first.

🕯️ 4. Invitations to Gatherings

From time to time, the House of Follent may summon its companions for:

  • Cellar door or off-site tastings
  • Blend previews or release celebrations
  • Fellowship gatherings where story, wine, and good company meet

Yearly Commitment

To keep the Fellowship simple and sustainable, membership is structured as a yearly commitment, billed annually.

The Membership Charter (In Brief)

The full Charter of the Founding Fellowship is written in the language of crests, vineyards, and medieval oaths — but its heart can be shared simply.

In Joining the Fellowship, You:

  • Are recognised as one of the first companions of Château Follent
  • Receive an annual allocation — your Chapter Release
  • Hold a permanent place in the Ledger of the Founders (if you wish your name recorded)
  • Are invited to share in the ongoing story of the House, in seasons of planting, pressing, and pouring

The House of Follent, In Turn, Commits To:

  • Honour the original privileges of the Founding Fellowship, even as future clubs or circles may be formed
  • Treat this first company of members with enduring gratitude and priority
  • Craft wines with care, integrity, and imagination, worthy of the Fellowship that bears our name

The Ledger of the Founders

The House of Follent shall keep a Ledger of the Founding Fellowship, inscribed with the names of all twenty-five companions who take their place in this inaugural circle.

This ledger is more than a list; it is a record of gratitude and remembrance.

Those inscribed will be remembered as:

“Guardians of the First Vintage,
And honoured members of the Founding Fellowship of Château Follent.”

How to Express Your Interest

Places in the Founding Fellowship are strictly limited to 25.

If you would like to express interest in joining the Founding Fellowship:

  1. Register your interest using the contact form on this site, or
  2. Email the House of Follent with the subject line: Founding Fellowship

Please let us know:

  • Whether you are seeking a 6-bottle or 12-bottle allocation
  • Whether you would like your name recorded in the Ledger of the Founders
  • Any notes about how you first came to know Château Follent

Should a place be available, you will receive a reply with next steps, allocation details, and timelines.

Closing Words

The Founding Fellowship is more than a club. It is a shared promise that the story of Château Follent will be written alongside friends.

To those who join us at the beginning:

“So it is written.
So it shall stand.
Welcome to the Founding Fellowship of Château Follent.”

Founder note

One vintage at a time

I started Chateau Follent out of curiosity; first as a way to learn more deeply about wine, and then as a small garagiste project shaped by experimentation, community, and craft.

This conversation on The Wined Up podcast is part of that story. We talked about making wine and mead, learning through the Sydney Amateur Winemakers Club, and what it means to build something slowly, one vintage at a time.

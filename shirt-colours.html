How to Use

Share this page with your shirt maker and specify that each colour must stay cool-toned to harmonise with Lord James Follent's Deep Winter palette anchored by Deep Navy (`#1B2452`) and Deep Charcoal (`#232328`).

Ask for high-contrast pairings for formal wear (white, midnight navy, jet black) and subtle texture or twill for mid-tone blues, greys, and silvers to keep the shirts visually refined.
